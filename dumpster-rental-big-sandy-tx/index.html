Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are allowed in dumpsters?

We accept wood, metal, concrete, yard debris, household items, and construction materials. Certain hazardous materials like asbestos, chemicals, and paints are restricted.

How long can I keep a dumpster rental?

Standard rentals range from 7 to 14 days. Extensions are available upon request with flexible scheduling.

Do I need a permit for placement?

Permits may be required for street placement. Our team can handle permit applications and guide you through local requirements.

How does delivery and pickup work?

You schedule delivery, clear the area, load your debris, then request pickup when done. Many companies allow you to be off-site during delivery and pickup.

What happens if I exceed the weight limit or fill line?

You may incur overage fees or the rental pickup may be refused until the load is corrected.

Big Sandy is a town in Upshur County, Texas, United States. As of the 2020 census, the town's population was 1,231. A lake of the same name is cut nearly in half by U.S. Highway 80, the main thoroughfare of Big Sandy. It lies directly west of the larger cities of Gladewater and Longview.
